What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment Analysis is a natural language processing (NLP) technique used to determine whether textual data is positive, negative, or neutral. In the context of food delivery services, it helps identify customer satisfaction, areas for improvement, and emerging trends in feedback.

Key Features

AI-Based Model

Uses machine learning to classify reviews into positive, negative, and neutral categories.

Bulk File Analysis

Upload Excel or CSV files and get instant sentiment reports for all entries.

Insights & Trends

Track how sentiment evolves over time and identify key customer pain points.
